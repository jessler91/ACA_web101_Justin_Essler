Bacon #1 is Floppy Bacon

What constitutes floppy bacon? I decided not to attempt to smoke the bacon (about half the bacon in Britian is smoked; the unsmoked product is sometimes called "green" bacon), mostly because I don't own a smoker and my dim memories of eating bacon in the UK didn't conjure up a smoky flavor.
